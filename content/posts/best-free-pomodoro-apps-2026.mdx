There are hundreds of Pomodoro apps. Most are basic countdown timers with a tomato icon. A few actually help you get work done.

## What Makes a Good Pomodoro App?

Before the rankings, here's what separates a useful Pomodoro app from a glorified kitchen timer:

- **Task tracking** — knowing what you worked on, not just that you "focused"
- **Customizable durations** — because 25 minutes isn't ideal for everyone
- **Ambient sounds** — built-in focus music or noise, so you don't need extra tabs
- **Progress tracking** — daily goals, streaks, or analytics to maintain momentum
- **Offline support** — works without internet
- **No friction** — instant access, no mandatory account, no ads interrupting sessions

With that in mind, here are the best free Pomodoro apps in 2026.
